About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science, University at Albany, SUNY. I am grateful to work under the supervision of Dr. Chinwe Ekenna in Robotics Algorithms and Computable Systems (RACS) lab. My research interest lies in Robotics and Automation and Applied Mathematics. I developed two robot motion planning algorithms integrating machine learning techniques during my master's program. My current research involves the development and designing of a topological path planner for robots/biomolecules with application in robot motion planning and computation biology.
